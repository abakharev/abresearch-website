01Services

Four ways we make your Moldflow programme more predictive.

Each engagement is scoped against a measurable outcome — a closed correlation gap, a Synergy workflow that runs unattended, a surrogate model that hits target accuracy on your part family.

01 / 04
Solver API

Custom Moldflow Solver APIs

Plug-in physical models, custom boundary conditions, and post-processors that run inside the Moldflow solver itself — the integration path designed and shipped at Autodesk by our principal.

02 / 04
Synergy

Synergy automation & tooling

Automated study setup, batch DOE runs, and standardised reporting inside Moldflow Synergy. Typical engagement: 60–80% reduction in analyst hours per project and a workflow your team owns.

03 / 04
Materials

Material property calibration

When the Moldflow material card doesn't match what's in your press, we fit the constitutive model to your rheology and PVT data — closing the correlation gap that blocks first-shot success.

04 / 04
ML

Machine learning surrogates

Trained on your simulation runs to explore design spaces in seconds rather than days — feed optimisers, support design reviews, and quote with confidence before the full 3D solve completes.
